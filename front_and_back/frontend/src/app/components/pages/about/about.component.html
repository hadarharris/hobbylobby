About Us

🌟 Our goal is to connect people with shared interests and expand their circle of friends.

Users register on the website and provide a brief biography where they specify hobbies and areas of interest.

Using similar characteristics, the system recommends other users who are similar to them. 🌍

Moreover, users can be exposed to groups with different areas of interest that might intrigue them.

You don't need to search - it finds you.

🧩 Automatic presentation of groups and people with similar areas of interest to yours.

In one group - most or all of your areas of interest.

An algorithm-based application designed to link people based on their areas of interest. 💡
